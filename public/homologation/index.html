Medical License Recognition — Approbation Guide

Solvia guides you through the entire Approbation (medical license recognition) process in Germany, Austria, and Spain. Whether you're an MBBS graduate from India, a médico from Argentina or Colombia, or a doctor from any country worldwide, we provide personalized roadmaps tailored to your qualifications.

How the Process Works

  1. Assessment — We analyze your medical degree, country of origin, and language level to determine your pathway.
  2. Document Preparation — Get a personalized checklist of all required documents: apostilled diploma, translations, language certificates, and more.
  3. Application — We handle communication with authorities (Regierungspräsidium, Ärztekammer, etc.) on your behalf.
  4. Exam Preparation — If you need the Kenntnisprüfung or FSP, we prepare you with specialized courses.
  5. License Issued — Receive your Approbation and start practicing medicine in Europe.
